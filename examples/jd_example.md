# Senior Backend Engineer — Payments Platform

We are hiring a senior backend engineer to own services in our payments
platform. You will design and operate high-throughput, idempotent APIs that
move money between accounts, build new fraud-prevention controls, and mentor
two mid-level engineers on the team.

## Requirements

- 5+ years building production backend systems
- Strong Python (FastAPI / asyncio) **or** Go
- Postgres at scale — schema design, indexing, query tuning
- Experience designing idempotent, fault-tolerant APIs
- Working knowledge of a major cloud provider (AWS preferred)
- Comfortable being on-call for services you own

## Nice to have

- Prior fintech / payments / ledger experience
- Kafka or another streaming platform
- Experience mentoring or leading small teams
- Familiarity with PCI DSS or SOC 2 controls

## About the team

Five engineers, two PMs, growing to ten by year-end. We deploy ~20 times a
week and run our own oncall rotation.
